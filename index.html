Sampurna Pal_

I am currently a PhD student in the Theoretical Statistics and Mathematics Unit at the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata under the guidance of Prof. Ritabrata Munshi. I completed my Master of Mathematics from the Indian Statistical Institute, Bangalore . Before that, I completed my Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from IIEST, Shibpur.

I work on analytic number theory, primarily using the delta method to study various properties of \(L\)-functions.

Research Interest

  • \(L\)-functions
  • Delta Method
  • Subconvexity Problem
